About the Founder & Vige.Dr

Vige.Dr is a proprietary technology startup founded in 2021, operating at the intersection of veterinary science and decentralized technology. Our mission is to modernize global pet data management through scalable SaaS ecosystems.

The startup was founded by Dr. R. Vijayakumar, a highly qualified professional holding a Master of Veterinary Science (M.V.Sc.) in Veterinary Medicine. Dr. Vijayakumar is an expert Android App Developer and a recipient of a Gold Medal in startup pitching.

By leveraging Hedera Hashgraph (Web3) infrastructure, Vige.Dr ensures that veterinary health records are immutable, secure, and easily accessible. Vige.Dr is proud to be part of the Google for Startups Cloud Program, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in pet-tech.
